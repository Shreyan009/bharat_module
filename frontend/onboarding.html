Terms & Conditions

Expand and review each section, then check to confirm your understanding. You must acknowledge all 8 sections to continue.

1. Blind Shipping & Brand Neutrality Protocol

Click to expand · Read and confirm your understanding.

1.1. All products dispatched via a Bharat Modules purchase order must be shipped in Blind Packaging. This means the external and internal packaging must avoid any OEM-specific branding, logos, or identifying marks unless otherwise specified for technical compliance (e.g., part numbers or safety stamps).

1.2. The OEM is strictly prohibited from including the following within or upon any shipment:

  • Company brochures, business cards, or marketing flyers.
  • OEM-direct invoices, price lists, or thank you notes.
  • QR codes or URLs leading to the OEM's private website or social media.
  • Direct contact information for the OEM's sales or support teams.

1.3 Exclusions: Branding on components, logos engraved on product are excluded from blind shipping.

2. Communication & Direct Contact Rules

Click to expand · Understand documentation and communication guidelines.

2.1. The only documentation permitted within the shipment is the Bharat Modules Packing Slip and the required Technical Data Sheets & compliance documents. Any invoice provided to the carrier for logistics purposes must list Bharat Modules as the Shipper of Record.

2.2. Any attempt to reach a Bharat Modules customer for direct business, or to encourage them to bypass the platform for future orders, constitutes a material breach of contract. This will result in termination of the OEM's account, loss of any pending payouts and a permanent ban from the website.

3. Product Quality & Packaging Requirements

Click to expand · Confirm your obligations to product integrity.

3.1. The OEM warrants that all components supplied are new, authentic, and strictly conform to the technical specifications and Proof of Origin declarations provided during the RFQ phase.

3.2. The OEM is solely responsible for industrial-grade packaging sufficient for long-haul transport.

  • The 48-Hour Rule: If a product arrives damaged, the Customer has 48 hours to report it.
  • OEM Liability: If damage is due to inadequate packaging or factory error, the OEM must provide a replacement at no additional cost to the Customer or Bharat Modules, including expedited shipping.

4. Documentation Compliance & Defect Resolutions

Click to expand · Acknowledge your liability towards documentation and defects.

4.1. Since Bharat Modules serves high-stakes sectors (Aerospace/Defense), Quality includes documentation.

  • If a part is physically perfect but arrives without the promised Certificates, the product is considered to be Non-Compliant.
  • The OEM has 3 business days, after required time to get certifications to provide missing digital documentation. Failure to do so has to face the strike rule.

4.2. In cases of confirmed manufacturing defects, the OEM agrees to:

  • Initiate a replacement within the time as discussed.
  • Accept a full refund deduction from their Bharat Modules account if a replacement cannot be provided within the project deadline.

5. Payment & Escrow Process

Click to expand · Understand payout timelines and escrow.

5.1. To protect the ecosystem, Bharat Modules acts as the financial intermediary.

  • These funds are held in a Bharat Modules account and are only released to the OEM upon successful dispatch or completion of delivery.
  • The payments are processed within 15 to 30 business days, starting from the delivery date for Pay on Delivery orders or from the dispatch date for prepaid orders to OEM's registered bank account.

6. Milestone Payments & Adjustments

Click to expand · Review policies on custom order milestones and deductions.

6.1. Milestone Releases (For Custom Orders): For high-value manufacturing (e.g., custom Aerospace components), payments may be split:

  • Milestone A: OEM will be paid the required amount to procure the raw materials.
  • Milestone B: The remaining amount is released only after the order is dispatched or delivered.

6.2. Bharat Modules will deduct the agreed-upon platform commission and any applicable logistics fees before the final payout. In the event of an OEM-side error (returns/damages), Bharat Modules reserves the right to imply penalty.

6.3. Changes in payment terms: Manufacturer will contact Bharat Modules for a custom payment terms & will be implemented after mutual acceptance.

7. Order Cancellation Terms

Click to expand · Detailed rules around dispatch limits and non-cancelable items.

7.1. The OEM agrees to the following cancellation windows, acknowledging the difference between commodity hardware and specialized engineering:

  • Standard Components: The OEM must accept a no-penalty cancellation before order dispatch.
  • Custom/Bespoke Components (CNC, Aerospace, Tooling, 3D printing): Once the OEM has started production, the order is considered Non-Cancellable. The OEM is entitled to the material-milestone payment, but must cease further labor if a cancellation request is received.

8. Returns, Refunds & Logistics Liability

Click to expand · Understand your obligations in case of returns.

8.1. Returns are strictly limited to Technical Non-Conformity.

  • Definition of Non-Conformity: Any instance where the physical part does not match the dimensions, material grade, or tolerances specified in the RFQ, or where the "Proof of Origin" documentation is missing or wrong.
  • OEM Obligation: Upon a verified claim of Non-Conformity, the OEM is contractually obligated to provide a replacement within the original lead-time window or issue a full refund at the Customer's discretion.

8.2. Financial Recovery & Refund Processing:

  • Refund policy: If the manufacturer is fully paid before delivery & a valid refund is initiated by the customer (within 48 hours of delivery), the manufacturer is responsible for total refund.
  • Logistic Liability: If a return is triggered by OEM error (wrong part/failed audit), the OEM is responsible for the cost of reverse logistics and the shipping of the replacement part.

8.3. The OEM acknowledges that Bharat Modules performs technical verification and lead generation for every transaction.

  • If a refund or return is processed due to OEM negligence (manufacturing defect, documentation failure, or shipping error), Bharat Modules will retain its full Service Fee (margin). The OEM will be responsible for the difference, ensuring the Customer is made whole while Bharat Modules is compensated for the technical labor expended on the failed transaction.

The Bharat Modules manufacturer onboarding process is a secure four-step verification journey designed for Indian OEMs and industrial component manufacturers. To become a Bharat Verified manufacturer and list your products on India's closed B2B procurement network, you must complete all four phases: profile registration, document upload, terms and conditions acknowledgement, and mutual non-disclosure agreement signing.

In Step 1, you verify your registration profile by confirming your business name, email address, and phone number via OTP. You also select the component categories that best represent your manufacturing output, including propulsion systems, power and energy components, avionics and navigation hardware, command and communication devices, airframes and structures, and payloads and actuators. In Step 2, you upload the required compliance documents including your GST registration certificate, MSME certificate, company bank statement, factory or manufacturing license, and company PAN card. All documents must be in PDF format and under 5 MB.

Step 3 requires you to read and acknowledge all eight sections of the Bharat Modules Platform Terms and Conditions covering blind shipping protocols, communication rules, product quality requirements, documentation compliance, payment and escrow processes, milestone payment terms, order cancellation policies, and returns and logistics liability. Step 4 covers the Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement (M-NDA), which governs the confidential exchange of business information between Bharat Modules and your organisation. Upon signing, your onboarding application is submitted for review. Our internal audit team verifies all documents within two business days, after which you receive full access to the Manufacturer Dashboard.
