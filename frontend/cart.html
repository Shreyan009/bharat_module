The Bharat Modules RFQ Cart is a secure portal for enterprise buyers, defence procurement teams, and system integrators to compile and submit Requests for Quotation (RFQ) for verified Indian-manufactured industrial components. Once you have browsed the product catalogue and selected the components you need, you can add them to your RFQ cart with the required quantities and submit a single consolidated quotation request directly to our sourcing team.

All products available through the Bharat Modules RFQ process are sourced exclusively from Bharat Verified and MSME Certified Indian OEM manufacturers. This means every component in your RFQ is backed by a Certificate of Conformity, a Technical Data Sheet, and Proof of Origin documentation. Pricing is subject to NDA and bulk rates are available on request. Once your RFQ is submitted, our procurement team reviews your requirements and contacts you within 24 hours with competitive manufacturer quotes.

The RFQ cart supports multiple products in a single submission. You can adjust quantities, add more products from the catalogue, or remove items before submitting. After submission, you will receive confirmation at your registered work email address, and the full RFQ history is available in your Buyer Dashboard under My RFQs. Bharat Modules handles the entire procurement communication on your behalf, ensuring full supply chain confidentiality and compliance through our closed B2B industrial network.
