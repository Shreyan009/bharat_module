Bharat Modules is India's closed-network B2B platform exclusively for verified Indian manufacturers and OEMs. If you manufacture industrial components, defence hardware, drone parts, aerospace modules, or any indigenous product in India, you can list your verified inventory on our platform and receive direct Request for Quotation (RFQ) submissions from enterprise buyers and defence procurement teams. Our platform eliminates the need to attend expensive trade expos, rely on cold-calling, or depend on import traders and middlemen.

To register as a manufacturer on Bharat Modules, your business must hold an active GST registration, a valid MSME certificate, and provide verifiable proof of manufacturing. Import traders, shell companies, and re-packagers are not permitted on the platform. Only genuine Indian manufacturers who produce components indigenously are eligible for listing. This strict verification process — called the Bharat Verified standard — ensures that every buyer on our platform trusts that your listing represents a real, compliant, original manufacturer.

Once verified and listed, your full product catalog is hosted live 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, giving your business 365 days of visibility to qualified procurement teams. You will receive formatted, high-intent RFQs directly into your dashboard without any cold outreach required. Our transparent pricing model includes a one-time onboarding fee for compliance and catalog setup, an annual subscription for platform access and priority buyer visibility, and a minimal success fee applied only when a verified purchase order is confirmed.
